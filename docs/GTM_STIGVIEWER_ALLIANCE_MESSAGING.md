# STIGViewer Alliance - Go-to-Market Messaging Framework

**Strategic Principle**: "Never Outshine the Master"

**Date**: 2026-01-05
**Objective**: Position KHEPRA as **allied partner**, not competitor or replacement

---

## The Positioning Challenge

**What STIGViewer Fears**:
- Vendor trying to replace them
- Vendor diminishing their value proposition
- Vendor capturing their customer relationships
- Vendor controlling their product roadmap

**What We Need**:
- Access to their 100,000+ user base
- Integration with their established platform
- Co-marketing opportunities
- Referral revenue or partnership deals

**The Balance**: Make STIGViewer look **more valuable** by partnering with us, not threatened.

---

## Core Messaging Framework

### ❌ **AVOID** - "Transformation" Language

**Never Say**:
- "KHEPRA transforms STIGViewer..."
- "Turn STIGViewer into a multi-framework platform..."
- "STIGViewer was STIG-only, now it's..."
- "We fix STIGViewer's limitations..."
- "STIGViewer users need KHEPRA to..."

**Why This Fails**: Implies STIGViewer is incomplete/inferior/needs fixing.

---

### ✅ **USE** - "Complementary Extension" Language

**Always Say**:
- "KHEPRA extends STIGViewer's native capabilities..."
- "STIGViewer users can now leverage existing STIG data for..."
- "We enrich the data STIGViewer already displays..."
- "Built specifically to work seamlessly with STIGViewer..."
- "STIGViewer remains the authoritative compliance platform, KHEPRA adds..."

**Why This Works**: Positions STIGViewer as the core, KHEPRA as the enhancement.

## Objection Handling Guide

### Objection 1: "This makes STIGViewer look incomplete"

**Response**:
> "Not at all. STIGViewer is the complete STIG compliance solution - that's why we built KHEPRA to integrate with it. Think of it like spell-check for Microsoft Word: Word is complete, but spell-check makes documents better. STIGViewer is complete; KHEPRA makes STIG data more useful for audits that require CMMC or NIST mappings."

---

### Objection 2: "We could build this ourselves"

**Response**:
> "You absolutely could - and if you do, we'd love to license our 36,195+ control mappings to accelerate your roadmap. Building this library took us 18 months and cost $500K+ in expert labor (mapping 28,639 STIG controls to CCI/NIST/CMMC). Partnering with us gets your users this capability **today** without diverting your engineering resources from core STIGViewer features."

---

### Objection 3: "What if you become a STIGViewer competitor later?"

**Response**:
> "We have zero interest in building a STIG viewer. STIGViewer already does this perfectly, and your 100K user base proves it. Our core competency is **post-quantum cryptography** and **compliance mapping algorithms** - not STIG workflow interfaces.
>
> **Analogy**: We're like Cloudflare (CDN) partnering with AWS (cloud platform). Cloudflare doesn't want to be AWS; AWS doesn't want to be Cloudflare. Both win by integrating."

---

### Objection 4: "Why not sell KHEPRA directly to our users?"

**Response**:
> "We could, but that creates a worse experience for users: they'd need to learn **two** tools (STIGViewer for STIG, KHEPRA for everything else). By partnering, users stay in **one** tool (STIGViewer) and get enhanced data. Lower training costs, higher adoption.
>
> **Plus**: You get 20-30% referral revenue with zero engineering lift. We handle support, updates, and sales - you collect checks."
