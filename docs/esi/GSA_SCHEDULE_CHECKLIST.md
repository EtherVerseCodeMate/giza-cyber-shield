# GSA Schedule 70 Application Checklist

**Target:** GSA Schedule 70 (Information Technology)
**Timeline:** 4-6 months from application to award
**Est. Cost:** $15K-$30K (consultant + legal)

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Application Preparation

### Business Registration (Week 1-2)

- [ ] **Register in SAM.gov** (System for Award Management)
  - Required for all federal contracting
  - Takes 7-10 business days to activate
  - Update annually
  - URL: https://sam.gov

- [ ] **Obtain CAGE Code** (Commercial and Government Entity)
  - Assigned automatically during SAM registration
  - Required on all GSA Schedule documents
  - Verify code is active in SAM

- [ ] **Obtain DUNS Number** (Data Universal Numbering System)
  - Now called UEI (Unique Entity Identifier)
  - Required for SAM registration
  - Free from Dun & Bradstreet
  - URL: https://www.dnb.com/duns-number.html

- [ ] **Register for GSA eOffer/eMod**
  - Portal for GSA Schedule submissions
  - Requires SAM registration to be complete
  - URL: https://eoffer.gsa.gov

- [ ] **Verify Financial Stability**
  - 2+ years of financial statements
  - Positive cash flow demonstration
  - Credit check may be required
  - Consider: GSA may require performance bond

### Legal & Compliance (Week 2-4)

- [ ] **Review GSA Schedule 70 Solicitation**
  - Download current solicitation from GSA.gov
  - Review all terms and conditions
  - Identify applicable Special Item Numbers (SINs)
  - Note: Solicitation updates annually

- [ ] **Select Applicable SINs**
  - [ ] 54151S: IT Professional Services
  - [ ] 54151HEAL: Health IT Services
  - [ ] 54151: Cybersecurity and Mobility Services
  - [ ] 54151HACS: Highly Adaptive Cybersecurity Services (HACS)
  - [ ] 54151CMO: Continuous Monitoring as a Service (CMaaS)

- [ ] **Engage GSA Schedule Consultant** (Recommended)
  - Cost: $10K-$20K
  - Increases approval likelihood
  - Reduces back-and-forth with GSA CO
  - Recommended firms:
    - GovCon Advisors
    - GSA Schedule Consultants
    - SmallGovCon
    - RSM Federal

- [ ] **Retain Legal Counsel**
  - Specialized in government contracts
  - Review terms and conditions
  - Negotiate with GSA Contracting Officer
  - Cost: $5K-$15K

### Technical Documentation (Week 3-6)

- [ ] **Prepare System Security Plan (SSP)**
  - Document NIST 800-171 controls (110 controls)
  - Include network diagrams
  - Data flow diagrams
  - Encryption methods
  - Access control policies

- [ ] **NIST 800-171 Self-Attestation**
  - Complete self-assessment
  - Document all 110 controls implementation
  - Create Plan of Action & Milestones (POAM) for gaps
  - Attestation letter from CEO/CTO

- [ ] **Prepare Security Documentation Package**
  - Incident response plan
  - Disaster recovery plan
  - Business continuity plan
  - Data breach notification procedures
  - Supply chain risk management plan

- [ ] **Section 508 Compliance** (if applicable)
  - Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1 AA)
  - VPAT (Voluntary Product Accessibility Template)
  - Document accessibility features
  - Test with screen readers

## Phase 2: Pricing Structure Development

### Commercial Sales Practices (CSP) Analysis (Week 4-6)

- [ ] **Document Commercial Sales**
  - List all commercial customers (non-government)
  - Document pricing for each customer
  - Show pricing discounts and rationale
  - Prove you sell to commercial market

- [ ] **Determine Most Favored Customer (MFC)**
  - Identify customer receiving best pricing
  - Calculate discount percentage
  - This becomes basis for government discount
  - Government must receive equal or better pricing

- [ ] **Create GSA Pricing Matrix**
  - Base pricing (commercial list price)
  - GSA discount off commercial (typically 15-40%)
  - Volume tier discounts (optional)
  - Multi-year discounts (optional)

### Example GSA Pricing Structure for SouHimBou AI:

```
Product: SouHimBou AI Enterprise

Commercial List Price: $200,000/facility/year

GSA Schedule Pricing:
- Base GSA Discount: 25% off commercial = $150,000/year
- Volume Tier 1 (1-10 facilities): $150,000/facility/year
- Volume Tier 2 (11-50 facilities): $135,000/facility/year (10% additional)
- Volume Tier 3 (51+ facilities): $120,000/facility/year (20% additional)

Multi-Year Discounts:
- 1 year: Standard pricing
- 3 year commitment: 5% additional discount
- 5 year commitment: 10% additional discount

Most Favored Customer: [Redacted Commercial Customer] receives 28% discount
GSA receives equal or better: 25% + volume + multi-year = up to 40% total
```

- [ ] **Industrial Funding Fee (IFF)**
  - GSA charges 0.75% IFF on all sales
  - Include in pricing or absorb as cost
  - Paid quarterly based on reported sales

- [ ] **Price Realism Check**
  - Ensure prices are competitive with other GSA vendors
  - Research comparable products on GSA Advantage!
  - Price too high = no sales; too low = GSA rejects

### Financial Documentation (Week 5-6)

- [ ] **Prepare Financial Statements**
  - Last 2 years of financial statements (audited preferred)
  - Balance sheets
  - Income statements
  - Cash flow statements

- [ ] **Calculate Contract Line Item (CLIN) Structure**
  - Each product/service = separate CLIN
  - Pricing for each CLIN
  - Minimum order threshold (typically $100-$500)

- [ ] **Determine Contract Minimums**
  - Minimum order amount (GSA requires $2,500 typical)
  - Maximum order amount (typically $1M, can be higher)

## Phase 3: GSA Schedule Application (Week 7-12)

### eoffer Submission (Week 7-8)

- [ ] **Complete GSA eOffer Form**
  - Company information (from SAM)
  - Selected SINs
  - Product/service descriptions (500 words each)
  - Pricing for each CLIN
  - Terms and conditions acceptance

- [ ] **Upload Required Documents**
  - [ ] Commercial Sales Practices (CSP) Worksheet
  - [ ] Price List/Catalog
  - [ ] Financial statements (2 years)
  - [ ] System Security Plan (SSP)
  - [ ] NIST 800-171 self-attestation
  - [ ] Section 508 VPAT (if applicable)
  - [ ] Commercial customer list
  - [ ] Past performance references (3-5)

- [ ] **Write Product/Service Descriptions**
  - Clear, jargon-free language
  - Focus on government benefits
  - Include technical specifications
  - Highlight compliance features (NIST 800-171, CMMC, STIG)
  - 500 words max per product

#### Example Product Description:

```
SouHimBou AI Enterprise - STIG-First Compliance Autopilot

SouHimBou AI automates CMMC, STIG, and RMF compliance for defense contractors
and DoD installations. Our platform provides:

• Automated STIG validation for 300+ DISA STIGs
• CMMC Level 2/3 continuous compliance monitoring
• Risk Management Framework (RMF) ATO acceleration
• Post-quantum cryptography (NIST-approved algorithms)
• Real-time security posture dashboards
• Immutable audit trail using directed acyclic graph (DAG)
• NIST 800-171 automated evidence collection
• C3PAO assessment preparation

Technical Specifications:
• Deployment: Cloud (AWS GovCloud) or On-Premises
• Architecture: Multi-tenant SaaS with tenant isolation
• Security: FedRAMP-ready, NIST 800-171 compliant
• Integration: REST API, STIG Viewer (.CKL), SCAP, OVAL
• Scalability: 10 to 10,000+ endpoints per installation

Ideal for: Defense contractors pursuing CMMC certification, DoD installations
requiring STIG compliance, and organizations needing RMF authorization support.

Deliverables: Software license, installation support, training, documentation,
and ongoing technical support with 99.9% uptime SLA.
```

- [ ] **Submit eOffer Application**
  - Review all sections for accuracy
  - Ensure all documents uploaded
  - Digital signature from authorized official
  - Submit through GSA eOffer portal

### GSA Review & Negotiation (Week 9-20)

- [ ] **Initial GSA Review (2-4 weeks)**
  - GSA Contracting Officer (CO) assigned
  - Initial review of completeness
  - May request additional documentation
  - Respond to all requests within 5 business days

- [ ] **Negotiations (4-8 weeks)**
  - [ ] Pricing negotiations
    - GSA may challenge pricing if not competitive
    - Provide justification for pricing
    - May need to increase government discount

  - [ ] Terms & Conditions
    - Review GSA's proposed T&Cs
    - Negotiate any company-specific terms
    - Some terms are non-negotiable (federal law)

  - [ ] Technical Requirements
    - Address any security concerns
    - Clarify product capabilities
    - Provide additional documentation if needed

- [ ] **Final Revisions (1-2 weeks)**
  - Incorporate all negotiated changes
  - Submit final offer via eMod
  - CO reviews and approves

- [ ] **Contract Award (1-2 weeks)**
  - GSA issues contract number (GS-35F-XXXXX)
  - Contract uploaded to GSA Advantage!
  - Effective date established (typically 30 days from award)

- [ ] **Optimize for Search**
  - Use relevant keywords (CMMC, STIG, RMF, cybersecurity)
  - Select correct product categories
  - Add tags and filters
  - Enable comparison features

- [ ] **Set Up Ordering Information**
  - Points of contact (POC)
  - Customer service phone/email
  - Ordering procedures

### Compliance & Reporting Setup (Week 22-23)

- [ ] **Register for 72A Reporting**
  - Report all GSA sales quarterly
  - Pay Industrial Funding Fee (0.75%)
  - Set up accounting system integration
  - Deadline: 30 days after quarter end

- [ ] **Set Up Contract Administration**
  - Designate contract administrator
  - Set calendar reminders for:
    - Quarterly sales reporting (72A)
    - Annual contract renewal
    - Price increase requests (if needed)
    - Modification requests (eMod)

- [ ] **Train Sales Team**
  - How to sell on GSA Schedule
  - GSA ordering procedures
  - Contract terms and limitations
  - Prohibited practices (kickbacks, gratuities)

## Critical Success Factors

### Do's ✅

- **Be Patient:** Process takes 4-6 months, sometimes longer
- **Hire Consultant:** Worth the investment for first-time applicants
- **Price Competitively:** Research comparable GSA Advantage! listings
- **Respond Quickly:** GSA COs expect 2-5 business day response times
- **Document Everything:** Maintain audit trail of all commercial sales
- **Start Early:** Begin NIST 800-171 compliance NOW (longest pole)

### Don'ts ❌

- **Don't Lowball Pricing:** GSA will reject unrealistically low prices
- **Don't Skip Consultant:** 60-70% rejection rate for first-time DIY applicants
- **Don't Fake Commercial Sales:** GSA audits; fraud = debarment
- **Don't Miss Deadlines:** Slow response = application rejection
- **Don't Ignore 72A Reporting:** Quarterly reporting is MANDATORY
- **Don't Oversell Capabilities:** Describe products accurately
