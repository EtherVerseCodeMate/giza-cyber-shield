## 5. How to Order

### Ordering Methods

#### Method 1: Direct GSA Schedule Order

**For orders under Simplified Acquisition Threshold (SAT):**

1. Visit GSA Advantage!: https://www.gsaadvantage.gov
2. Search for "SouHimBou AI" or Contract Number: GS-35F-[TBD]
3. Add products to cart
4. Submit purchase requisition
5. Receive order confirmation within 2 business days

**Typical Timeline:** 5-10 business days from order to delivery

---

#### Method 2: ESA Direct Order

**For enterprise-wide or component-wide deployments:**

1. Contact Single Point of Contact (see Section 9)
2. Discuss requirements and scope
3. Receive custom quote (within 3 business days)
4. Submit order via:
   - Direct agency order (Military Interdepartmental Purchase Request - MIPR)
   - Contracting Officer purchase order
   - Government Purchase Card (GPC) if under cardholder limit
5. Execute Enterprise License Agreement
6. Receive license keys and onboarding schedule

**Typical Timeline:** 10-20 business days from order to deployment

---

#### Method 3: BPA/IDIQ Call Order

**If your agency has an existing Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) or IDIQ with GSA:**

1. Issue call order against BPA/IDIQ
2. Reference GSA Schedule contract GS-35F-[TBD]
3. Use pre-negotiated pricing (may differ from base GSA)
4. Submit through agency contracting office
5. Vendor responds within 5 business days

**Typical Timeline:** Varies by BPA/IDIQ terms
