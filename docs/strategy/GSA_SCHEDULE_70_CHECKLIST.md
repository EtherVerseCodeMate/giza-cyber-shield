# GSA Schedule 70 Application Checklist
 
**Target:** GSA Schedule 70 (Information Technology)
**Timeline:** 4-6 months from application to award
**Est. Cost:** $15K-$30K (consultant + legal)
 
---
 
## Phase 1: Pre-Application Preparation
 
### Business Registration (Week 1-2)
- [ ] **Register in SAM.gov** (URL: https://sam.gov)
- [ ] **Obtain CAGE Code** (Assigned during SAM registration)
- [ ] **Obtain UEI** (Unique Entity Identifier, replaces DUNS)
- [ ] **Register for GSA eOffer/eMod** (URL: https://eoffer.gsa.gov)
- [ ] **Verify Financial Stability** (2+ years of statements)
 
### Legal & Compliance (Week 2-4)
- [ ] **Review GSA Schedule 70 Solicitation**
- [ ] **Select Applicable SINs**
  - 54151S, 54151HEAL, 54151, 54151HACS, 54151CMO
- [ ] **Engage GSA Schedule Consultant** (Recommended: GovCon Advisors, RSM Federal)
- [ ] **Retain Legal Counsel**
 
### Technical Documentation (Week 3-6)
- [ ] **Prepare System Security Plan (SSP)** (110 NIST 800-171 controls)
- [ ] **NIST 800-171 Self-Attestation**
- [ ] **Prepare Security Documentation Package** (IRP, DRP, BCP)
- [ ] **Section 508 Compliance** (WCAG 2.1 AA, VPAT)
 
---
 
## Phase 2: Pricing Structure Development
 
### Commercial Sales Practices (CSP) Analysis (Week 4-6)
- [ ] **Document Commercial Sales** (List, pricing, discounts)
- [ ] **Determine Most Favored Customer (MFC)**
- [ ] **Create GSA Pricing Matrix** (Base, volume tiers, multi-year)
 
### Financial Documentation (Week 5-6)
- [ ] **Industrial Funding Fee (IFF)** (0.75%)
- [ ] **Price Realism Check** (Research GSA Advantage!)
- [ ] **Prepare Financial Statements** (2 years)
 
---
 
## Phase 3: GSA Schedule Application (Week 7-12)
 
### eOffer Submission (Week 7-8)
- [ ] **Complete GSA eOffer Form**
- [ ] **Upload Required Documents** (CSP, Price List, Financials, SSP)
- [ ] **Write Product/Service Descriptions** (Clear, government-focused)
 
### GSA Review & Negotiation (Week 9-20)
- [ ] **Initial GSA Review** (2-4 weeks)
- [ ] **Negotiations** (4-8 weeks - Pricing, T&Cs, Technical)
- [ ] **Final Revisions** (1-2 weeks)
- [ ] **Contract Award** (GS-35F-XXXXX)
 
---
 
## Phase 4: Post-Award Setup (Week 21-24)
 
### GSA Advantage! Listing (Week 21-22)
- [ ] **Create GSA Advantage! Storefront**
- [ ] **Optimize for Search**
- [ ] **Set Up Ordering Information**
 
### Compliance & Reporting Setup (Week 22-23)
- [ ] **Register for 72A Reporting** (0.75% IFF quarterly)
- [ ] **Set Up Contract Administration**
- [ ] **Train Sales Team**
 
### Marketing & Outreach (Week 23-24)
- [ ] **Update Website** (GSA Badge, Landing Page)
- [ ] **Create Government Marketing Collateral**
- [ ] **Outreach to Government Buyers**
